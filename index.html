Core Values

Our Vision

To make logistics and transportation easy, safe and efficient through the use of technology and a multimodal channel strategy.

Our Value

We are committed to Integrity, Professionalism, Transparency and Creativity.

Who We Are

Truckka Logistics Limited (TLL) is a team of professionals with rich and diverse corporate executive
backgrounds that is focused on aggregating infrastructure and technology solutions to bring
efficiency into the African logistics industry.
Truckka Logistics Limited will be migrating from diesel-engine trucks to Compressed Natural Gas (CNG)
powered trucks as part of our efforts to reduce global carbon emissions.

Our Services

Air Fright Services

As freight-aggregators, low-volume high-value cargo time-demand will be across by our air-freight Partners once agreements are reached and operations commence.

Road Haulage Services

Truckka Logistics Limited offers Road Haulage (Spot/Contract Operations) through the use of heavy-duty Client-Dedicated CNG/Diesel Trucks and 3rd Party Trucks all over the country.

Marine-Cargo Transportation Services

We also provides Cargo/Truck transfer services via the waterways using tug-boats and self-propelled barges and chartered multi-purpose vessels.

Rail Road Services

Truckka Logistics Limited will move high-volume (>600MT) low-cost cargo once the refurbished railway tracks along the Lagos-Kaduna/Kano axis is completed and our wagon-acquisition request is approved by Nigerian Railway Corporation (NRC) authorities.

Private Terminal Operations

Truckka Logistics Limited is developing one of the largest Private Terminals in Africa with the acquisition of a 10,000 Hectare land along Lagos-Badagry Waterways.
